- `[project.scripts]` contains `carta` and `carta-mcp` only.
- `carta.hook.hook:main` is absent.

- File: `carta/hook/hook.py`, lines 194–198

- `False` routes to `sys.exit(0)` — no injection (fail **closed**).
- Requirement HOOK-05: "Judge timeout fails open (injects result)."
- This is a logic bug independent of the wiring issue above.
